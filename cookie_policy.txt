# 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and activity.

We may also use:

* Local storage
* Session storage
* Web beacons / pixels
* Analytics scripts

All are treated as “cookies” in this policy.
