# WellNourish AI

**Personalized Wellness, Powered by AI**

WellNourish AI is a full-stack application designed to help users achieve their health and fitness goals by providing personalized diet and workout plans. Leveraging the power of advanced AI, the app generates tailored recommendations based on individual user profiles, preferences, and objectives.

## Core Features

- **Secure User Authentication**: Sign up and log in safely using Supabase Auth.
- **Personalized Profile**: Input your age, height, weight, fitness goals, dietary preferences, activity level, preferred cuisine, allergies, and medical conditions.
- **AI-Powered Plan Generation**: Get dynamic diet and workout plans generated based on your unique profile.
- **Plan Display**: View your current diet and workout plan in a clean, readable Markdown format.
- **Plan History**: Access previously generated plans to track your journey.
- **Responsive Design**: Enjoy a seamless experience across desktop and mobile devices.
- **Modern UI**: Aesthetically pleasing interface built with modern UI components.

## Disclaimer

WellNourish AI provides AI-generated diet and workout plans for informational purposes only. The information provided is **not** intended as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

**Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider** with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or before undertaking any new health regimen. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read or received from this application.

Reliance on any information provided by WellNourish AI is solely at your own risk.
