## ✨ Features

### 🧠 AI-Powered Personalization
Advanced algorithms analyze your preferences, goals, and dietary needs to create the perfect plan just for you using Google's Generative AI.

### 🍽️ Custom Meal Plans
Get weekly meal plans with detailed recipes, nutritional info, and grocery lists tailored to your cuisine preferences.

### 💪 Workout Routines
Complementary exercise plans designed to work with your nutrition goals for maximum results.

### 📊 Progress Tracking
Monitor your journey with intuitive dashboards and celebrate milestones along the way.

### 🛡️ Allergy Safe
We take allergies seriously. Every plan respects your dietary restrictions and sensitivities.

### ⚡ Time-Saving
No more endless searching. Get complete plans in seconds, not hours.

### 🔐 Secure Authentication
Built-in authentication with Supabase including email/password, magic links, and password recovery.
